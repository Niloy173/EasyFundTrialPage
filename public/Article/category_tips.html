How to raise funds according to your project category?

Community and Disaster

Under this category, it is very recommendable to add frequent updates in your project, explaining about the community you want to support and how committed you are to this cause, sharing the latest news and activities.

Share it through social media like Facebook to reach a bigger audience, and contact the people directly related to the community, to ask for their participation. Also, you can set an example by supporting your own project, to show that you are actively cooperating with your cause!

Community and Disaster

  • Use big social media to share your project like Facebook in order to reach a bigger audience
  • Add updates very frequently to show your commitment to the cause
  • Try to support your own project to set an example for others

Family and Medical

The perfect starting point for you, is to share your project with your closest people. Explain to them how they can help, step by step, and ask them also to share your project with their own friends. Asking for financial help can be awkward, but don’t forget that the people that love you are the people that will help you no matter what.

There will always be people that can relate to your story around the world. That’s why it is very important to share your project through social media. Many people could collaborate with you, if they have a chance to do it.

Family and Medical Key Point

  • Start sharing with people that already know about you and your problem
  • People who love you will be your best supporters: don’t be shy to share your project with them
